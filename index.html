What are copyright-free images?

Copyright-free images are images that are not protected by intellectual property laws, such as copyright, and are free to be used and modified by anyone. These images are often made available under licenses such as Creative Commons or are in the public domain.

Where can I find copyright-free images?

There are many websites that offer copyright-free images, such as:

  • Pexels
  • Unsplash
  • Pixabay
